chicken biryani recipe

simple and delicious chicken biryani recipe you can try at home.

ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • salt taste
  • lemon juice

steps:

  1. marinate chicken:
    • mix chicken with yogurt,lemon.Rest 30 mintues
  2. cook rice:
    • boil rice with whole spices untill 80% done.
  3. cookchicken:
    • saute onion,add tomatoes,then chicken. cook till done.
  4. layer biryani:
    • in pot: layer chicken,rice,mint/coriander,saffron milk
  5. steam(Dum)
    • cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 mintues.
